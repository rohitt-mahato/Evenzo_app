About the Event

Prepare for a three-day journey into the cutting edge of electronic music. The Electronic Beats Festival returns for its 10th anniversary, bringing together the world's most innovative DJs, producers, and visual artists for an unforgettable weekend.

Set in a newly renovated industrial complex, the festival features three distinct stages, each with its own unique sonic and visual identity. From deep techno in the concrete bunker to melodic house in the open-air pavilion, expect world-class sound design and immersive installations.
